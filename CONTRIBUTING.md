# Contributing to Awesome AI for Scientific Research

Thank you for your interest in contributing!

## Scope

This list is specifically focused on **AI systems that automate the scientific research process**. A project must fit at least one of these criteria:

- Autonomous AI agents that conduct end-to-end research (ideation → experiments → writing)
- Systems that automate hypothesis generation or research idea discovery
- Deep research agents that synthesize scientific knowledge
- Automated paper writing, review, or academic productivity tools
- Literature review and paper search automation
- Domain-specific AI accelerating drug discovery, materials science, biology, etc.
- Benchmarks and evaluation frameworks for AI research automation

❌ **Not in scope**: General LLM tools, chatbots, image generators, trading bots, coding assistants (unless specifically for research automation).

## How to Contribute

1. Fork this repository
2. Add the repository to the correct category in `README.md`
3. Follow the existing format: `| [**owner/repo**](url) | ⭐ stars | \`Language\` | description |`
4. Sort entries within each category by star count (descending)
5. Submit a Pull Request with a brief explanation of why the project belongs here

## Quality Standards

- Must be directly related to automating scientific research
- No duplicates
- No archived or abandoned repositories (unless historically significant)
- Descriptions should be informative and concise
